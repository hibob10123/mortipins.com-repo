About Morti-Site

Morti-Site is a Brawl Stars fan website connected to the YouTube channel MortipinS. It’s built for players who enjoy quick minigames, useful stats, and community moments.

What you can do here

  • Play minigames like Brawldle and TrophyGuess.
  • Explore stats for players, clubs, events, and brawlers.
  • Read blog posts about updates, tips, and community projects.
  • Submit clips (and help shape future clip-rating features).

Community clips

Clips submitted by users are community content. We expect submitters to own the rights to what they submit (or have permission), and to follow our Community Guidelines.

If you believe a clip or page violates your rights or the rules, please see the Contact page for how to reach us.

Not affiliated

Morti-Site is a fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Supercell.
