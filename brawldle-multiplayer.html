Brawldle Multiplayer (Beta)

Experience Brawldle in real-time with other players! Our multiplayer mode pits you against an opponent where you both watch the same clips and compete to guess the ranks faster and more accurately.

How Multiplayer works

  • Matchmaking: Join the queue and we'll find an opponent for you.
  • Rounds: You'll go through a series of clips together.
  • Scoring: Points are awarded based on how close your guess is to the actual rank.

As this is currently in Beta, we are constantly working to improve the experience. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please join our Discord and let us know!
