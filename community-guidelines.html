Community Guidelines

These rules help keep Morti-Site safe, fun, and AdSense-friendly. If you submit clips or participate in the community, follow these guidelines.

Allowed content

  • Family-friendly Brawl Stars gameplay clips.
  • Educational guides and strategy content.
  • Funny moments that don’t rely on hate, harassment, or explicit content.

Not allowed

  • Hate or harassment: targeting people for race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, etc.
  • Sexual content: nudity, sexual acts, sexually explicit content.
  • Violence / gore: graphic injury, threats, or promotion of violence.
  • Illegal activity: anything that promotes or depicts illegal acts.
  • Spam: repeated submissions, misleading links, or attempts to manipulate ratings/leaderboards.
  • Copyright infringement: do not submit clips you do not own or have permission to share.
  • Personal info: doxxing, sharing private information, or encouraging harassment.

Clip submission rules

  • Submit only clips you created or have permission to share.
  • Keep it safe for general audiences (avoid profanity-heavy overlays or explicit audio).
  • No misleading titles/links. If it’s a YouTube link, it should be the actual clip.

Enforcement

Content may be removed and accounts may be restricted if rules are violated. If you believe content breaks the rules, please report it via the Contact page.
