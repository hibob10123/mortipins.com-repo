How to Play Brawldle

Brawldle is a Brawl Stars guess the rank game built on real community-submitted clips. Watch the gameplay, pick a rank — Bronze through Masters — and see how you stack up. Two ways to play: grind Elo in Unlimited, or one shared Daily clip with community stats.

Unlimited

Elo ranking & leaderboard

  • Watch & guess the rank from the clip—Bronze through Masters.
  • Elo scoring: correct guesses add points; wrong guesses subtract. As your Elo rises, gains get smaller and losses hurt more.
  • Leaderboard: compete globally on Elo.
  • Account: sign in to save your score and show up on the board.

Daily

One clip per UTC day

  • One clip, one guess per day—same clip for everyone.
  • Community chart after you submit: see how your guess compares (no Elo in Daily).
  • Come back tomorrow for a new featured clip.
