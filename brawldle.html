How to Play Brawldle

Unlimited Mode (Elo Ranking)

This is where you climb the leaderboard!

  • Watch & Guess: You'll see a gameplay clip. Guess the player's rank in that clip.
  • Ranks: The possible ranks are: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Mythic, Legendary, and Masters.
  • Scoring (Elo System):
    • Correct Guess: Gain Elo points.
    • Incorrect Guess: Lose Elo points.
    • The Challenge: As your total Elo score increases, you'll gain fewer points for correct guesses and lose more for incorrect ones. This makes reaching higher Elo tiers a true test of skill!
  • Leaderboard: Compete for the top spot! Your Elo score is tracked on the global leaderboard.
  • Login to Save: Remember to log in to save your Elo points and appear on the leaderboard.

Daily Game Mode

A fresh challenge every day!

  • One Clip, One Guess: You get one featured clip per day.
  • No Points, Just Pride: Guess the rank. There's no Elo awarded in this mode, but you can see how your guess compares to others with a distribution chart!
  • Come Back Daily: A new clip awaits you each day.

Ready to play? Choose your mode and show off your Brawldle expertise!
