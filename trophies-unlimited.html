About TrophyGuess

TrophyGuess is a unique challenge where you estimate a player's total trophies based on their gameplay. It requires a deep understanding of the Brawl Stars progression system and how players at different trophy levels behave.

Scoring and Hearts

  • Within +/- 7500 trophies: Earn 1 point.
  • Within +/- 2500 trophies: Earn 3 points and gain 1 heart (extra life).
  • Too far off: You lose a heart! The game ends when you run out of hearts.

Use the slider to select your trophy estimate. Pay attention to brawler levels, skins, and the complexity of the plays to get the best score!
