Site Update: Leaderboards & New Stats

A quick overview of what’s new and how to use the improved Stats experience.

We’ve been improving the site’s stats and competitive features so it’s easier to find useful info quickly — and so leaderboards feel cleaner and more reliable.

What changed

  • Stats sections are organized into collapsible panels so you can focus on what you need.
  • Player + Club lookup are designed to be fast and readable.
  • Events + Brawlers sections load dynamic information and keep the page content-rich.

How to use the Stats page

Visit the Stats page and expand the section you want: Player Stats, Club Stats, Active Events, Upcoming Events, Brawlers, or the Global Leaderboard panel.

Tip: If you’re on mobile, expanding only one section at a time keeps scrolling smooth and makes the page easier to navigate.

Leaderboards

Leaderboards are evolving. As we refine score integrity and anti-abuse protections, we’ll keep updating the display and the rules so it’s fair and fun.

What’s next

  • More blog posts (guides, patch notes summaries, and community highlights)
  • Better clip discovery + a simple rating flow
  • UI polish for cleaner, consistent design across pages
